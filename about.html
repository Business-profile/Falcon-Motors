IN REMEMBRANCE

Our Founder - Late. Aijaz Hussain

"Excellence in service comes from passion, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to quality."

Falcon Motors was founded by the visionary Late. Aijaz Hussain, whose passion for automotive excellence and commitment to customer satisfaction laid the foundation of what we are today. With decades of experience in the automotive industry, he built this workshop on the principles of integrity, quality, and trust.

His dedication to providing exceptional service and his meticulous attention to detail earned him the respect and loyalty of countless customers throughout Kota and beyond. Late. Aijaz Hussain believed that every vehicle deserves the best care, and every customer deserves honest, reliable service.

Today, Falcon Motors continues to honor his legacy by maintaining the same high standards of service excellence, technical expertise, and customer care that he established. His vision guides us every day as we serve our community with the same passion and dedication that he embodied throughout his life.
